About Us

We are The Supreme Rulers, a dynamic and diverse EVE Online corporation. Our mission is to dominate the universe through mastery of every category of gameplay, including:

  • Mining: Gather resources to fuel our conquests.
  • Exploration: Discover new frontiers and ancient secrets.
